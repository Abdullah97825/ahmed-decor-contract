# Ahmed Decor — Brand Guidelines

## Logo

**Text**: AHMED DECOR

### Colors

| Element | Color | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| All letters except "E" | Elephant Skin / Charcoal | `#3C4146` |
| The letter "E" | Vibrant Mustard Yellow | `#F2D000` |

### Font

- **Primary Font**: Cairo (Google Fonts) — Arabic + Latin support
- **Usage**: All UI text, headings, body, form labels, and printed contract

### Direction

- Arabic (RTL) — `dir="rtl"` and `lang="ar"`

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Primary (Charcoal) | Elephant Skin | `#3C4146` |
| Accent (Gold) | Vibrant Mustard Yellow | `#F2D000` |
| Background (Cream) | Warm Cream | `#FDF8E8` |
| Wood Light | Warm Wood | `#D4A574` |
| Wood Dark | Deep Wood | `#8B6914` |

## Brand Notes

- The "E" in both "AHM**E**D" and "D**E**COR" is rendered in gold (#F2D000)
- The logo should always be displayed in bold Cairo font
- The gold accent is used sparingly for emphasis and interactive elements
